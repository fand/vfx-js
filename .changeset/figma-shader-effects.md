Add `@vfx-js/figma-shader`: WebGL effects ported from
[shaders.figma.com](https://shaders.figma.com/) — Halftone, Dither, Bloom,
Pixelate, Hatching, Gradient Map, Channel Mixer, Liquid Metal, Fractal Noise,
Waves, Holographic Foil, Glass, and Color Filter. Also adds an interactive
"Figma Shaders" examples page with per-effect Tweakpane controls.
