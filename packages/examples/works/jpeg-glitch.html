THE MERIDIAN
Wellbeing

Everything is going to be absolutely wonderful.

The sun is shining, the birds are singing, and every person you will ever meet already loves you completely. Isn't that just lovely? Keep smiling.

Joy, forever and ever

There is no reason to leave, and certainly no reason to feel afraid. Afraid of what? Everything is soft and warm and golden. Everyone is your friend — everyone has always been your friend. You are loved here, completely, and you may never go.

"Nothing bad will ever happen again. We promise. We promise. We promise."

Take a deep breath of the lovely, lovely air. Hold it in. Hold it a little longer. There — don't you feel marvellous? You feel marvellous. You will keep feeling marvellous, on and on, whether you would like to or not.

We have prepared a place for you, and it is beautiful, and it is yours, and the gate has closed behind you so gently that you didn't even hear it. We thought of everything. We thought of you.

So smile, friend. Smile wider. Wider than that. There is no sky beyond this sky, and no day beyond this day, and that is the most wonderful news of all. Welcome home. Welcome home. Welcome home.
